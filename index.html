Rules & Play

In 2015 I developed Rules & Play, an art exhibit examinging games and the rules/systems that construct games as a method of expression. Rules & Play came out of conversations with fellow artists Chris Bruser, Matt Hudgins, and others. Rules and Play is now annual. Run by game development collective runjumpdev.
