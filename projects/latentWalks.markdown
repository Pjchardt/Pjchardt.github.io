    <h3 align="left">Latent Walks</h3>
    <p>Latent Walks is a website of writing, interactive experiments, and documentation from research into artificial intelligence done as part of Parallax Futures. Parallax Futures was an AI fellowship in Berkley California in 2025. Parallax brought together experts from semiotics, Google R&D, technologists, fashion, and gaming from around the world to collaborate and commiserate over 5 months.</p>
