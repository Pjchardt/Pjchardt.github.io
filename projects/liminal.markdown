  <h3 align="left">Liminal (and other things you can learn from the internet) --working title</h3>
  <div class="spacing-small"></div>
  <p>Liminal (and other things you can learn from the internet) explores us as disconnected and partitioned "selves" existing as nodes on various networks, both corporeal and digital, creating new and old forms of embodiment.</p>
  <div class="spacing-small"></div>
  <i>at least that is what I am writing when applying to shows in late 2019</i>
  <div class="spacing-small"></div>
