    <h3 align="left" style="color: #abfa95;">IVE</h3>
    <p style="color: #abfa95;">Color field art generator. Participants stand at one of three stations. Each station controls three variable properties of the art generation software. Through communication, and learning each stations impact on the generation, participants can exert influence on the generation. Each station's form is a playful alien made out of vacuum formed acrylic with exposed electronics inside surrounding a structural form lit by LEDs. The generated imagery is projected unto a surface facing the three stations. Hardware and software support by Steve Dominick and Mike Schmidt.</p>
